Salmon Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb salmon fillet, cut into chunks
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 cup green beans (sitaw), trimmed
  • 1 cup water spinach (kangkong)
  • 2-3 tablespoons tamarind paste (or fresh tamarind)
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the onion and tomatoes, and simmer for about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the radish and cook for another 5 minutes until slightly tender.
  3. Stir in the tamarind paste and green chili peppers. Adjust the sourness to your liking.
  4. Add the salmon chunks and cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through.
  5. Add the green beans and cook for an additional 3 minutes.
  6. Finally, add the water spinach and cook for another minute. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Cooking Tips:

For a richer flavor, you can add a bit of shrimp paste (bagoong) while cooking.

About Sinigang

Sinigang is a beloved Filipino dish known for its sour and savory flavor profile. Traditionally made with various meats and vegetables, it has evolved over the years, with salmon becoming a popular choice due to its rich taste and health benefits.
